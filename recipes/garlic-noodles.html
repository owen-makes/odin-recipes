San Francisco Garlic Noodles

Description

Garlic Noodles are a San Francisco staple, according to chef J Kenji Lopez Alt.

This simple dish will surely blow your mind with its deep garlicky and umami flavors,

and it's a must cook if you like fusion cuisine

Ingredients

  • 250g Noodles
  • 30g oyster sauce
  • 30g soy sauce
  • 30g fish sauce
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 6 cloves fresh garlic
  • 30g Parmesan cheese
  • 25g green onions or scallions
  • 1 g red chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles to al dente according to package instructions
  2. Combine oyster sauce, soy sauce and fish souce in a small bowl
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant
  4. Add the prepared sauce to the garlic butter. Bring to simmer
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the simmering sauce. To thicken the sauce, add some of the noodle water
  6. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, green onions (or scallions) and red chili flakes
  7. Serve on a bowl and enjoy!
